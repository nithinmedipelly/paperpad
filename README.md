PaperPad is a voice-first notebook for people who'd rather talk than type. Tap one big button, speak naturally in any of 30+ languages, and your words become tidy bullet points on screen. Spoken numbers are written as digits automatically — "twenty-five point nine nine" becomes 25.99, "दो लाख पचास हज़ार" becomes 250000. Build tables hands-free by dictating the column names, then each cell. When you're done, save as PDF, Word, or plain text, or share straight to WhatsApp, Email, or SMS. Designed especially for older users — big fonts, three simple buttons, sky-blue paper-and-ink palette. Installs as a Progressive Web App on Android and iPhone, works offline, no account required.
